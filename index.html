Disclaimer

This simple calculator intends to help people estimate how much import taxes they can expect to pay when ordering goods from abroad or when they return home with purchased goods from their international travels. It is entirely possible that this tool is wrong for many reasons, so there is no guarantee that the shown price is actually what you will pay. South Africa has different tariffs for different countries depending on the bi-lateral Trade Agreements and this tool incorporates that to estimate import duties. A certificate of origin is applicable for imported goods from countries that have Trade Agreements with South Africa in order to benefit from lower import tariffs. Please visit SA Customs and Excise for more information.
